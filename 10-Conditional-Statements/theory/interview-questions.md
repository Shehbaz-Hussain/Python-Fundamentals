# Interview Preparation Tips

- Understand the purpose of each conditional statement rather than memorizing syntax.
- Practice tracing program execution step by step.
- Be comfortable predicting the output of small programs.
- Know the difference between assignment (`=`) and comparison (`==`).
- Understand when to use `if`, `if-else`, `if-elif-else`, and nested `if`.
- Practice writing programs without referring to notes.
- Test your programs with different input values to verify every execution path.
