# Problem 1 — Monthly Salary Calculator

Write a program that asks the user for:

- Hourly wage
- Hours worked per day
- Working days per month

Calculate and display:

- Daily salary
- Monthly salary
- Annual salary

### Formula

```text
Daily Salary = Hourly Wage × Hours per Day

Monthly Salary = Daily Salary × Working Days

Annual Salary = Monthly Salary × 12
```

# Problem 2 — Student Percentage Calculator

Ask the user to enter marks obtained in five subjects.

Calculate:

- Total marks
- Percentage
- Average marks

Assume:

```text
Maximum marks per subject = 100
```

### Formula

```text
Percentage = (Obtained Marks / Total Marks) × 100
```

# Problem 3 — Electricity Bill Estimator

Input:

- Units consumed
- Price per unit

Calculate:

- Total bill
- Government tax (12%)
- Final payable amount

### Formula

```text
Total Bill = Units × Price per Unit

Tax = Total Bill × 12 / 100

Final Bill = Total Bill + Tax
```
